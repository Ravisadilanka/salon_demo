Our Story

A Legacy of
Beauty & Grace

Founded by Premsri Hewawasam following a transformative scholarship in Japan in 1985, Scissors has grown into one of Colombo's most celebrated beauty destinations.

With multiple branches across Sri Lanka and thousands of loyal clients, we bring international techniques with a deeply personal Sri Lankan touch.
